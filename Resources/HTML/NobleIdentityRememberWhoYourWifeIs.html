Remember Who Your Wife Is

My wife is a part of the family of God. She has been chosen and been reconciled to God, and restored as a new creation in Christ Jess, she is a woman, made in His image and for His glory. She is flesh of my flesh and bone of my bone. She is a virtuous woman in Christ Jesus and her value is far above rubies. God has chosen her before the foundation of the world that she should be holy and without blame before Him in love. She has been adopted as a child by Jesus Christ to Himself, according to the good pleasure of His will, wherein He hath made her accepted in the Beloved. She has redemption through His blood, the forgiveness of sins, according to the riches of His grace that she should be to the praise of His glory. He is in her midst and is mighty to save and rejoices over her with shouts of joy. She is His bride and He is her bridegroom and I'm responsible to be God's unhindered conduit of His love. I'm called to cherish her so she can experience the love of God which casts out all her fears, so she can be without spot and wrinkle.

Philemon 1:6That the communication of the faith may become effectual by the acknowledging of every good thing which is in you in Christ Jesus."
