Grouped Character Qualities

Often, failures in our character manifest themselves in our lives in negative ways. If you are struggling in a particular area, identify it in the table below and touch it to jump to a list of Christ's qualities that you should study in order to overcome that failure.

The roots of sin:
  • Anger
  • Guilt
  • Lust
  • Bitterness
  • Greed
  • Fear

Anger

  • Humility
  • Joyfulness
  • Justice
  • Creativity
  • Sincerity
  • Reverence
  • Deference

Guilt

  • Security
  • Truthfulness
  • Discernment
  • Attentiveness
  • Responsibility
  • Thoroughness
  • Enthusiasm

Lust

  • Love
  • Self-control
  • Faith
  • Decisiveness
  • Alertness
  • Virtue
  • Loyalty

Bitterness

  • Forgiveness
  • Determination
  • Availability
  • Gentleness
  • Resourcefulness
  • Tolerance
  • Endurance

Greed

  • Generosity
  • Thriftiness
  • Initiative
  • Contentment
  • Patience
  • Hospitality
  • Gratefulness

Fear

  • Boldness
  • Meekness
  • Wisdom
  • Obedience
  • Persuasiveness
  • Endurance
  • Orderliness

Envy

  • Diligence
  • Sensitivity
  • Flexibility
  • Cautiousness
  • Dependability
  • Compassion
  • Discretion
