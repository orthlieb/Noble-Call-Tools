Wife As Listener

Lord,

I am willing to be made willing by Your grace. Give me the ear of the wise. Help me to understand my husband and live with him in understanding, that our prayers would not be hindered, and that we would be able to resolve every issue that comes up. Enable us to cast all these burdens on You. So Lord, I choose to be a willing woman. I’m scared. I’m afraid. I don’t know what I am going to face in this conversation. But Lord, give me the grace to be strong in the power of Your might.

Amen
