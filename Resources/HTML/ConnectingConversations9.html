Secrets to praying back to God all the truth He has revealed to you

If you fail to pray things back to God you will face the responsibility of truth on your own and experience one of the following consequences:
  1. Feel condemned by it and your heart will be hardened.
  2. Try to do it in the power of the ﬂesh and give up quickly.
  3. Mentally assent to it and become a hearer's of the Word but not a doer of the Word thereby deceiving yourself.

When you pray things back to God you are:

  1. Praying back to Him what He has shown you and acknowledging your need for Him to live through you.
  2. You are trusting God to complete that which He has begun in you.
  3. Recognizing our responsibility is to believe and speak with faith.
  4. You are accepting the reality that apart from God you can do nothing.
  5. You are accepting the fact that your power comes from containing Someone as a vessel versus trusting in ourselves and performing to become somebody in the power of the ﬂesh.
  6. You are changing your mind to agree with God. Repenting and turning away from your own way to follow God.
