Remember Who You Are

I am part of the family of God. I have been chosen and reconciled to God, and restored as a new creation in Christ Jesus. I am a woman, made in God's image and for His glory. I am flesh of my husband's flesh and bone of his bone. I am a virtuous woman in Christ Jesus and my value is far above rubies. God has chosen me before the foundation of the world that I should be holy and without blame before Him in love. I have been adopted by the Father through Jesus Christ, according to the good pleasure of His will wherein He hath made me accepted in the beloved. I have redemption through His blood, the forgiveness of sins, according to the riches of His grace, that I should be to the praise of His glory. He is my midst, mighty to save and rejoicing over me with shouts of joy. I am His bride and He is my Bridegroom and I am responsible to be the unhindered conduit of God's love to my husband. I am called to honor him, releasing the grace of God in his life, so that he can lead our family in paths of righteousness according to the Father's perfect will.
